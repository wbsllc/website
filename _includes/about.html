About

Worthington Behavioral Services LLC was founded out of a love for applied behavior analysis and a desire to support those providing services to individuals and families impacted by autism. WBS Founder, Jessica Worthington, BCBA, understands the importance of providing consistent, quality services to individuals with autism and recognizes the difficulties providers encounter when experiencing staffing shortages. At WBS, we value teamwork and strive to build a supportive team of professionals dedicated to enriching the lives of the autism community.

Let us support you in your mission to help those impacted by autism!
